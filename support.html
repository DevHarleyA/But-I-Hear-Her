Support

There are many ways to get support But I Hear Her!

They include:

"Share Your Story!" We seek audio accounts that share people’s experiences practicing caretaking during the Covid-19 pandemic. For information about how to share your story, see "Share Your Story!"

Donate materials. But I Hear Her can always use tablets, over-ear headphones, and other computers/screens to display audio stories/videos. To coordinate donation of materials, please email butihearher@gmail.com

Create art / music to accompany stories. We would love for our audio stories to have a music score and/or story art (for storytellers who desire this of their stories). If you would like to make art, music, dance or any other creative translations of stories please email us!

Donate to But I Hear Her. Our project has many expenses including web maintenance, electronic devices for displaying videos/audio stories, printing ourCreating With Kids! book, and more. Donations help cover these expenses and develop But I Hear Her’s future plans.

Create art / music to accompany stories. We would love for our audio stories to have a music score and/or story art (for storytellers who desire this of their stories). If you would like to make art, music, dance or any other creative translations of stories please email us!

Future plans include but are not limited to:

  • Building a digital But I Hear Her interface
  • Bringing But I Hear Her to new locations
  • Providing stipends for the project’s storytellers, translators, and artists
  • And many more!

To donate to But I Hear Her, please PayPal citizentalescommons@gmail.com or hwtrivilino@gmail.com and write “But I Hear Her donation” in the description field (donations sent via PayPal to citizentalescommons@gmail.com are tax-deductible). To donate by cash, check, or Venmo, please email butihearher@gmail.com.
